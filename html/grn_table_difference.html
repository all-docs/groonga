grn_table_difference

__attribute__((dllimport)) grn_rc grn_table_difference(grn_ctx *ctx, grn_obj *table1, grn_obj *table2, grn_obj *res1, grn_obj *res2)

Delete duplicated records in `table1` and `table2`.

ctx
The context object.
table1
The table.
table2
The table.
res1
Specify `table1`.
res2
Specify `table2`.

Returns GRN_SUCCESS on success, GRN_INVALID_ARGUMENT on `table1 != res1 || table2 != res2`.
